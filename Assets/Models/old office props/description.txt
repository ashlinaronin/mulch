A small collection of free, worn looking office props.
This pack includes:
- A metal desk
- swivel chair
- ceiling fan
- and customisable filing cabinet

The materials are a PBR gloss set up but can be converted to roughness by inverting 
the colours in the gloss map. (Ctrl-I in Photoshop) The gloss map is stored in the
alpha channel of the metalness map.
